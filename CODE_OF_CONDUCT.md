# Code of Conduct

## My goal of the project is to build a solid kernel with a welcoming community, the goal isnt to just fork TKT and be mad. the idea is to play around and learn.
* please be respectful and kind to others
* we dont allow ANY discrimination at all, this includes politics, sexuality, religion, ethnicity. just because someone is different doesnt mean we should say disrespectful things
* no slurs, at all. no excuses
